FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Is it really free to start? +
Yes. No monthly fees, no setup fees, no deposits. We only charge 20% of chargebacks we successfully recover for you, capped at $250 per dispute. If we don't win, you pay nothing.
What happens if you lose a dispute? +
You pay nothing. Zero. You're in exactly the same position as if you hadn't used our service. The merchant never loses money by using ChargeWon — that's the entire point of success-based pricing.
How long does setup take? +
About 60 seconds. Install from the Shopify App Store, connect your Stripe account with one click, and you're done. We immediately start monitoring for disputes and will handle any open chargebacks right away.
What types of chargebacks do you handle? +
All types: fraud claims (10.4), item not received (13.1), product not as described (13.3), credit not processed (13.6), and cancelled subscriptions (13.7). Our AI tailors evidence to each specific Visa/Mastercard reason code for maximum win probability.
Is my data secure? +
We connect via Stripe's official Connect OAuth — the same secure method used by thousands of apps. We never store card numbers or sensitive payment data. All API tokens are encrypted at rest with 256-bit AES encryption.
Do I need to do anything after installation? +
Nothing. ChargeWon runs fully automatically. We detect disputes, gather evidence, submit rebuttals, and collect our fee when we win. You can monitor everything from your dashboard, but zero action is required from you.
How is this different from Chargeflow? +
ChargeWon charges 20% capped at $250 per dispute vs. Chargeflow's 25% with no cap. We require no deposit (Chargeflow holds $100 upfront). We use true AI evidence generation rather than templates. And we're purpose-built for dropshippers and high-volume Shopify stores.
Can I cancel anytime? +
Yes. Since there's no monthly fee, there's nothing to cancel. Uninstall the app and we stop monitoring. Any disputes already in progress will be completed — we don't leave you hanging.
